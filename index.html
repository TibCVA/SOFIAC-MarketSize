≥ 10 M CAD Threshold – Verification

Size proxy embedded in stock filters (no additional haircut)

Threshold math
10 M CAD ÷ 450 CAD m⁻² ≈ 22 000 m² (building)
10 M CAD ÷ 15 M CAD PJ⁻¹ ≈ 0.67 PJ total; empirical 0.04 PJ yr⁻¹ site consumption matches CIB case studies.
All assets tabulated in Slide 2 already exceed these proxies; therefore the full $ 110 bn pool proceeds to adoption filters.

Strategic Conclusions

Ambition achievable with disciplined execution

SOFIAC must capture ~20 % of the accessible pool to deploy $ 1 bn by 2030 – realistic given its first-mover status and current CIB relationships.

Lock industrial anchor clients

Scale EaaS awareness
Use CIB showcase projects to accelerate tier-finance penetration beyond 30 %.

Expand provincial pipelines
Replicate QC-ON-AB playbook in BC and SK for incremental volume.
